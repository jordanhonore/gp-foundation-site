Where Healing Begins

We believe that every mother navigating the weight of postpartum depression, every child carrying the burden of trauma, and every family struggling to hold it together deserves access to real, licensed, compassionate care — regardless of income.

The G&P Foundation for Wellness funds therapy. Not someday. Now. Through our Care Scholarship Program, we pay licensed therapists directly so that families in Fort Bend and Harris Counties can receive the support they need without the impossible burden of out-of-pocket costs. We don’t just believe healing is possible. We fund it.

How It Works

📋

Apply

Families in Fort Bend and Harris Counties submit a simple, confidential application for a Care Scholarship to receive funded therapy sessions.

🤝

Match

Approved applicants are matched with a licensed therapist in our vetted provider network who fits their needs and preferences.

💚

Heal

The Foundation pays the therapist directly for 8–12 sessions, so families can focus entirely on healing — not on bills.

30–50 Care Scholarships in our first year
8–12 Therapy sessions funded per recipient
70%+ Of every dollar goes directly to care
$0 Dollars families pay out of pocket

Born from a Mother's Journey

The G&P Foundation for Wellness was born from lived experience — from a mother who knows firsthand what it feels like to need help and not be able to afford it. Our founder turned her pain into purpose, creating an organization dedicated to ensuring no family is denied mental health care because of their financial situation.

“I created this Foundation because no mother should have to choose between feeding her family and getting the help she needs. Healing isn’t a luxury. It’s a right.”
