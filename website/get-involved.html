Get Involved

Healing is a community effort. Here’s how you can help.

Fund a Family’s Healing

Every dollar you give goes further here. Because our facility overhead is donated by our partner, Good & Plenty Wellness, over 70 cents of every dollar you give goes directly to paying for therapy sessions. That’s not a marketing number. That’s our model.

$50

Covers one therapy session for a mother or child in need

$200

Funds a full month of weekly therapy for one family

$600

Sponsors a complete 12-session Care Scholarship

$5,000

Funds an entire cohort of 8–10 Care Scholarship recipients

Bring Your Gifts to the Village

We’re building something real in Fort Bend County, and we need people who believe in this mission as much as we do. Whether you’re a clinician, an organizer, a photographer, or simply someone who wants to help mothers and families thrive — there’s a place for you here.

  • Event Support — Help with community workshops, wellness events, and outreach
  • Administrative Support — Data entry, filing, communications assistance
  • Professional Services — Pro bono legal, accounting, marketing, or photography
  • Clinical Volunteers — Licensed therapists willing to provide discounted or pro bono sessions
  • Community Ambassadors — Spread the word in your neighborhood, church, or school

Institutional & Corporate Partnerships

The G&P Foundation for Wellness partners with hospitals, OB/GYN practices, school districts, faith-based organizations, and corporate sponsors who share our commitment to community wellness. Together, we can extend the reach of our Care Scholarship Program and ensure that more families receive the mental health support they deserve.

Whether your organization is looking to create a clinical referral pipeline, sponsor a scholarship cohort, integrate employee wellness programming, or co-host community events — we’re ready to build something meaningful with you.

Partnership opportunities include:

  • Clinical referral pipelines
  • Sponsored scholarship cohorts
  • Employee wellness integrations
  • Community event co-hosting
