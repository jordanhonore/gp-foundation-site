Events

Healing happens in community. Come as you are.

From somatic wellness workshops to maternal circles to community health fairs, our events are designed to bring families together in spaces that nourish both body and mind. All events are open to the community. Most are free or offered at subsidized rates. Childcare is provided whenever possible — because we know that’s often the difference between a mother being able to show up or not.

Recurring Events

MONTHLY 1st

Maternal Wellness Circle

First Saturday of Every Month | Free for All Mothers

A monthly gathering for mothers — new and seasoned — to come together in a facilitated, judgment-free space led by licensed clinicians and wellness practitioners.

BI-WKLY •

Community Somatic Wellness Workshop

Every Other Week | Free for Scholarship Recipients, $15 for Public

Mindful movement, Pilates-based conditioning, and somatic stress release in a supportive group setting.

QTRLY •

Family Wellness Day

Quarterly | Free Community Event

A day of resources, activities, wellness screenings, and connection for the whole family.

About
