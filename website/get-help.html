Get Help

You don’t have to carry this alone. We’re here.

If you or someone in your family is struggling with mental health and can’t afford care, the G&P Foundation may be able to help. Our Care Scholarship Program covers the full cost of licensed therapy for eligible individuals and families in Fort Bend and Harris Counties. Applying is simple, confidential, and free. There is no shame in needing support — only courage in reaching for it.

Am I Eligible?

  • Reside in Fort Bend or Harris County, Texas
  • Household income at or below 200% of the Federal Poverty Guidelines
  • Experiencing depression, anxiety, trauma, grief, or family conflict
  • Postpartum mothers with perinatal mood disorders (priority consideration)
  • Youth ages 12–18 experiencing trauma or behavioral challenges (priority consideration)

What You’ll Need to Apply

  • Proof of residency (utility bill, lease, or ID)
  • Income verification (tax return, pay stubs, or benefit letter)
  • Brief description of current mental health needs

Your information is kept strictly confidential. We never share your personal details with anyone outside of the clinical care team assigned to your case.

How to Apply

1

Apply Online

Complete our secure application form. It takes about 10 minutes.

2

Verification

Our team reviews your application and verifies eligibility within 5–7 business days.

3

Matching

We match you with a licensed therapist based on your needs, preferences, and availability.

4

Begin Healing

Your therapy sessions begin. The Foundation pays your therapist directly — you pay nothing.

Frequently Asked Questions

How much does this cost me?

Nothing. The Care Scholarship covers the full cost. No co-pays, hidden fees, or bills.

How many sessions will I receive?

8 to 12 sessions. Renewable if clinician determines continued need.

Is this really free?

Yes. We’re a 501(c)(3) public charity funded by donations and grants.

Will this affect my insurance?

No. Services are independent of insurance.

Can I choose my therapist?

We match based on your needs. You can specify preferences for gender, specialty, language, or cultural background.

I’m not sure I qualify. Should I still apply?

Yes. We review every application individually.

If You’re in Crisis Right Now

If you or someone you know is in immediate danger, call 911. For the 988 Suicide & Crisis Lifeline, call or text 988. For the Crisis Text Line, text HOME to 741741.
