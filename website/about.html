About the Foundation

A healing space for the families who need it most.

Our Story

The G&P Foundation for Wellness was born from a simple truth: too many families in our community are struggling with mental health challenges they can’t afford to address.

In Fort Bend and Harris Counties — two of the fastest-growing and most diverse regions in Texas — thousands of families fall into the “care gap.” They earn too much for Medicaid but too little for consistent out-of-pocket therapy. Mothers suffering from postpartum depression wait months for help. Children coping with trauma go unseen. Families unravel in silence.

We exist to close that gap. Not with programs that take years to launch or funding that gets lost in overhead. We fund therapy directly — paying licensed clinicians to provide real, evidence-based care to the families who need it now.

Our founder, a Licensed Marriage and Family Therapist, built her career while navigating her own pregnancies — learning firsthand how fragmented, inaccessible, and lonely the mental health system can be for women and families. She didn’t just imagine a better way. She built it.

Our Mission

To eliminate the financial barriers preventing underserved families from accessing quality mental and somatic healthcare — with a focus on postpartum mothers, at-risk youth, and families in crisis.

Our Values

Access Over Everything

Mental health care is not a luxury. We remove the single greatest barrier — cost — so that families can focus on healing, not finances.

The Whole Person

We don’t separate the mind from the body. Our programs integrate clinical therapy with somatic wellness because true healing addresses both.

Community as Medicine

Isolation is a symptom. Connection is the cure. We create spaces where mothers and families find belonging, not just treatment.

Every Dollar to Care

Over 70% of every dollar we raise goes directly to funding therapy sessions. Our hybrid model keeps overhead low and impact high.

How We Maximize Every Dollar

The G&P Foundation for Wellness operates a unique hybrid model in partnership with Good & Plenty Wellness, LLC. This structure allows us to deliver maximum impact with minimal overhead.

Good & Plenty Wellness donates clinical and studio space to the Foundation at no cost — eliminating one of the largest expenses nonprofits typically face. This means your donation goes where it matters: directly to care.

  • 70%+ of every dollar raised goes directly to funding therapy sessions for families in need.
  • Donated clinical and studio space from Good & Plenty Wellness keeps our facility costs at zero.
  • Independent board oversight ensures transparent governance, financial accountability, and mission alignment.

This model means that when you give to the G&P Foundation, you’re not paying for office leases or administrative bloat. You’re paying for a mother’s therapy session. A child’s healing. A family’s second chance.
